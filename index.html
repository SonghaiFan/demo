The Populace of Florida

Florida is the third-most populous state in the United States. Its residents include people from a wide variety of ethnic, racial, national and religious backgrounds. The state has attracted immigrants, particularly from Latin America.

Spanish is spoken by more than 20% of the state's population, with high usage especially in the Miami-Dade County area.
